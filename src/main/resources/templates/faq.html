Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery on orders placed before 4 PM within city limits.

Can I customize my bouquet?

Absolutely! You can share your preferences, and our team will craft a personalized arrangement just for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking for your convenience.

How are the flowers kept fresh during delivery?

Our bouquets are delivered in temperature-controlled packaging to ensure they arrive as fresh as when they left our studio.
